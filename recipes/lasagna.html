Lasagna

Description

The dish is a fluffy potatoe toping with meat, sauce and pasta underneath.

This is a delicious treat whenever you are feeling hungry or thirsty.

Ingredients

  • potatoe
  • meat
  • sauce
  • tomatoes
  • salt
  • garlic

Steps

  1. Cut up the meat
  2. Add the sauce to the meat
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes to the meat and sauce
  4. Add the garlic and sauce
  5. Add the salt
  6. Add the potatoes
  7. Heat in the over for 1 hour
