Pie

Description

This dish is a cripsy flaky round pie containing a delicious filling of red berries and blue berries topped with cream and sugar.

Ingredients

  • pastry
  • red berries
  • blue berries
  • cream
  • sugar

Steps

  1. Cut the pastry in two circles
  2. Mush up all the berries in a bowl
  3. Add the cream to the same bowl
  4. Add on pastry cicle to a new bowl
  5. Pour in the mushed berries and cream from the bowl
  6. Add the odd pastry circle to the top
  7. Place in a headed over for 40 minutes
  8. Sprinke the sugar on top
