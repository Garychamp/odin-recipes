Spagette

Description

This dish is a stringy special dish from the heart of Italy. It has a red sauce with stringy pasta that can also have shavings of cheese added for extra flavour.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • tomatoes
  • cheese
  • meat
  • garlic
  • butter

Steps

  1. Cut up the meat
  2. Add the tomatoe sauce to the meat
  3. Add the pasta to the meat and sauce
  4. Add the garlic and butter
  5. Heat in the over for 30 minutes
  6. Add the cheese using cheese grater
